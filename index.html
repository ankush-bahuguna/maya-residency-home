About Maya Residency Homestay

Your Ideal Refuge

Located in Adibadri, Chamoli, Garhwal, Maya Residency Homestay offers a tranquil retreat with mountain views, lake access, and various amenities. Since we first opened our doors, we have been committed to providing our guests with a comfortable and relaxing stay. Our locally-run accommodation is not just a place to stay, but a home away from home.

  • Local guidance and travel help
